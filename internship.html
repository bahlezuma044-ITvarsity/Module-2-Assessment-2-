THE INTERNSHIP

American comedy film directed by Shawn Levy, written by Vince Vaughn and Jared Stern, and produced by Vaughn and Levy. The film stars Vince Vaughn and Owen Wilson as recently laid-off salesmen who attempt to compete with much younger and more technically skilled applicants for a job at Google. Rose Byrne, Max Minghella, Aasif Mandvi, Josh Brener, Dylan O'Brien, Tobit Raphael, Tiya Sircar, Josh Gad, and Jessica Szohr also star.

Salesmen Billy and Nick find themselves unemployed in the digital world. In a bid to prove their competence, they land an internship at Google and try to beat some tech-savvy geniuses.

  • Initial release: 29 May 2013 (USA)
  • Director: Shawn Levy
  • Story by: Vince Vaughn
  • Box office: $93.5 million
  • Produced by: Vince Vaughn; Shawn Levy
  • Budget: $58 million
