FREE GUY

American science fiction action comedy film. The film stars Ryan Reynolds he is actually a non-player character in an open-world video game and decides to become the hero of the story and save his friends from deletion by the game's creator. Jodie Comer, Joe Keery, Lil Rel Howery, and Utkarsh Ambudkar also star.

MR BONES 2

It is the story of Hekule, the King of Kuvukiland who is given a gemstone by the dying Kunji Balanadin.It is up to Mr. Bones to cure his King and get rid of this cursed stone by travelling 130 years into the future, in the city of Durban.

Fast & Furious 8

The film stars Vin Diesel, Dwayne Johnson, Jason Statham and many more. In the film, Dominic Toretto has settled down with his wife Letty Ortiz, until cyberterrorist Cipher coerces him into working for her and turns him against his team, forcing them to find Dom and take down Cipher.

MR BEAN'S HOLIDAY

The film stars Rowan Atkinson in the title role, with Max Baldry, Emma de Caunes, Willem Dafoe and Karel Roden in supporting roles. In the film, Mr. Bean wins a holiday to Cannes, but on his way there accidentally causes a young boy to be separated from his father.

THE INTERNSHIP

Directed by Shawn Levy, written by Vince Vaughn and Jared Stern, and produced by Vaughn and Levy. The film stars Vince Vaughn and Owen Wilson as recently laid-off salesmen who attempt to compete with much younger and more technically skilled applicants for a job at Google.
